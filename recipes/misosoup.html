Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup made primarily of miso paste, dashi (broth), and additional ingredients such as vegetables, seaweed, and tofu.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Dashi (broth)
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of Seaweed
  • 2 Green Onions, sliced diagonally
  • 1 Carrot
  • Half package Tofu
  • 1/4 teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Truffle Powder
  • 3 tablespoons Red Miso Paste

    • Directions

    1. In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, combine dashi granules and water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer, and add carrot, seaweed, green onions, onion powder, garlic powder, truffle powder, and tofu. Whisk in the miso paste. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
